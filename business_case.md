# Business Case

## Objective

Build a proactive CPC intelligence layer for inbound ocean logistics.

## Problem

Transportation PMs, planners, finance partners, and analysts often spend significant time combining shipment milestones, carrier updates, and cost ledgers to explain CPC movement after costs have already occurred.

## Proposed Solution

Combine:

- Synthetic container-level logistics data
- CPC forecasting model
- Charge-code attribution
- Lane Intelligence Agent
- Root Cause Agent
- FastAPI service layer

## Expected Outcomes

- Earlier identification of CPC escalation risk
- Faster root-cause analysis
- Lane-level operational prioritization
- Reduced manual investigation effort
- Stronger collaboration across transportation, finance, and planning teams
