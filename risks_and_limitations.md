# Risks and Limitations

## Data Quality Risk

Missing milestones, delayed cost postings, and inconsistent charge-code mappings can reduce prediction quality.

## Explainability Risk

Transportation and finance teams need evidence behind recommendations. Black-box predictions are not enough.

## LLM Hallucination Risk

Agent responses must be grounded in validated data. The system should not generate unsupported explanations.

## Adoption Risk

Operational teams may not immediately trust AI-generated recommendations. Human-in-the-loop review is important.

## Real-Time Cost Risk

Continuous agents, retrieval pipelines, and model scoring can increase infrastructure cost. Business impact must justify scale.
